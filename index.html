COLLOQUIUM '23

CELEBRATING ENGINEERS' DAY

Colloquium '23 is a prestigious event that will be celebrated on September 15th, 2023, at the National Institute of Technology Andhra Pradesh (NIT Andhra Pradesh) in honor of Engineers' Day. This event is tailored for aspiring researchers and students who have conducted groundbreaking research and are eager to showcase their findings to a wider audience. Colloquium '23 offers a platform for participants to present their research through poster presentations and oral discussions.

Engineers' Day, celebrated on September 15th each year in India, commemorates the birth anniversary of Sir Mokshagundam Visvesvaraya, a distinguished engineer and statesman. Colloquium '23 is a fitting tribute to his contributions to engineering and a testament to the spirit of innovation that drives engineering students and researchers.

Event Highlights

Event Name: Colloquium '23

Date: September 15, 2023

Venue: To Be Announced (TBA), NIT Andhra Pradesh, Tadepalligudem, Andhra Pradesh, India

The heart of Colloquium '23 lies in the poster presentation session, where student researchers will exhibit their research work to a discerning audience. Participants are encouraged to submit their research abstracts and topics in advance, and accepted entries will be invited to create posters for display at the event.

In addition to the poster presentations, the event will feature oral presentations by the authors of the best research papers from each engineering department at NIT Andhra Pradesh. These presentations will delve deeper into the research and allow for interactive discussions with the audience.

Colloquium '23 will provide a conducive environment for networking and knowledge sharing. Attendees will have the chance to connect with fellow researchers, faculty, and industry experts, fostering collaboration and future research partnerships.

Outstanding poster presentations and oral presentations will be recognized with awards and certificates, acknowledging the excellence and contributions of the participants.
